FAQ

What buyers ask before they commit

What's re-export trade, and when should I use it?
It means routing your goods through Hong Kong before they reach their final market. Use it when a direct route is slower, pricier or impractical — it can trim duties, consolidate multi-origin orders, shorten effective lead times and re-document origin where appropriate. If a shipment keeps getting stuck or costing too much direct, this is usually the fix.
Which e-commerce channels can you run for me?
Amazon (including FBA inbound prep), eBay, TikTok Shop, Temu and your own direct-to-consumer store. We handle channel-specific packaging, labelling and fulfilment, so your listings stay compliant and your stock stays topped up.
Do you handle customs and the paperwork?
Yes — that's the part most people dread, and it's the part we own. We prepare commercial invoices, packing lists, bills of lading, certificates of origin and re-documentation as needed, provide HS classification support and coordinate clearance to each destination's rules.
What MOQ and trade terms do you offer?
MOQ flexes from small trial lots for e-commerce testing through to full-container loads for re-export cargo. We commonly work FOB, CIF, CFR and DDP, with the final terms confirmed in your quote based on product, destination and season.
How fast can I get a quote?
Usually within one business day. Send your product details, target markets, quantities and preferred trade terms — and a named account manager comes back with a costed plan, not a runaround.
