Service line 01

Entrepot / Re-export Trade

转口贸易

We route your goods through strategically chosen free-trade and transshipment hubs to optimize duties, consolidate cargo and shorten effective lead times. Beyond movement, we handle customs coordination, origin re-documentation and the trade finance that keeps deals flowing.

Whether you need to bridge two markets, consolidate multi-origin orders, or manage sensitive routing requirements, our team designs a compliant path that protects margins and timelines. You get a single, transparent landed-cost picture and a partner accountable for every leg of the journey.

Service line 02

Cross-border E-commerce Export

小商品电商出口

We take small commodities to global consumers and businesses across the channels that matter — Amazon, eBay, TikTok Shop, Temu and your own DTC store. From product sourcing to warehousing, B2C/B2B fulfilment and last-mile delivery, we run the operational backbone behind your online sales.

Our team helps you select winning products, prepare compliant listings and packaging, and stage inventory close to your buyers. Whether you ship FBA replenishment or fulfil orders one parcel at a time, we keep your store stocked, your costs predictable and your customers delighted.
