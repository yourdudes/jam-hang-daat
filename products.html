Service 01 · Re-export trade

Bridge two markets — without bleeding margin at customs

When a direct route is slow, costly or just won't work, send it through us. We route your goods via Hong Kong's free port to trim duties, consolidate multi-origin orders and shorten the lead time your buyer actually feels.

Behind the move, we handle the parts that trip people up: customs coordination, HS classification, certificates of origin, re-documentation and the trade finance that keeps deals flowing. You get one transparent landed-cost picture and one team on the hook for every leg.

Sell to the world. We'll run the back office.

Your buyers are on Amazon, eBay, TikTok Shop, Temu and your own store. We get your small commodities in front of them — sourcing, warehousing, B2C and B2B fulfilment, last-mile and returns, all under one roof.
