About us

We took the pain out of cross-border trade — and built a business on it

For over a decade, makers, brands and online sellers have handed us their hardest shipments. Here's who we are and why they keep coming back.

Who we are

Your goods, handled like they're ours

We're a Hong Kong trading team that does two things well: routing re-export cargo through one of the world's busiest free ports, and running the back office for sellers shipping small commodities to the world.

From our base in Kwun Tong, we pull sourcing, quality control, customs, warehousing and last-mile into a single service — so you deal with one team, one cost view and one person who owns the result. Faster to market, clearer landed costs, fewer 3am surprises.

Our story

Built one trade lane at a time

We started as a small re-export desk on the Hong Kong waterfront. Today we move cargo through dozens of lanes and run the back office for sellers across every major marketplace — but we still pick up the phone.

2013

A desk by the water

We opened as a specialist re-export desk, routing regional manufacturers' cargo through Hong Kong's free port.

2017

We went digital

Sellers asked us to handle their online orders too — so we built a cross-border e-commerce arm and onboarded our first marketplace stores.

2021

Warehouses near the buyer

We added overseas warehousing and FBA prep across 42 lanes, so stock sits close to demand and delivery gets faster.

2024

One view, end to end

Re-export and e-commerce now run on shared tracking, giving you a single window from quote to doorstep.
