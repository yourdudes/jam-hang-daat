About us

A borderless trade partner for a connected economy

Jam Hang Daat exists to make international trade feel effortless — pairing the discipline of entrepot logistics with the agility of digital commerce.

Company overview

Who we are

Jam Hang Daat is an international trading company focused on two complementary engines: entrepot / re-export trade and cross-border e-commerce export of small commodities. We help manufacturers, brands and online sellers reach global buyers without getting tangled in customs, routing or fulfilment complexity.

From our base near a major free-trade port, we orchestrate sourcing, quality control, hub routing, documentation and last-mile delivery as one continuous service. The result is faster time-to-market, clearer landed costs, and a single team accountable for the whole journey.

Our vision

Our values

Our history

Built lane by lane, channel by channel

What began as a small re-export desk has grown into a full borderless trade operation spanning physical hubs and digital marketplaces.

2013

The re-export desk

Founded as a specialist entrepot trade desk, routing goods through free-trade hubs for regional manufacturers.

2017

Going digital

Launched our cross-border e-commerce export arm, onboarding small-commodity sellers to global marketplaces.

2021

Network at scale

Expanded to 42 transshipment hubs and integrated overseas warehousing for faster last-mile fulfilment.

2024

One borderless platform

Unified entrepot and e-commerce operations under shared visibility tools, giving clients one view from quote to delivery.
