About us

We took the pain out of cross-border trade — and built a business on it

As a new Hong Kong trade and cross-border e-commerce company, we take on makers', brands' and online sellers' hardest shipments. Here's who we are and why they trust us.

Who we are

Your goods, handled like they're ours

We're a Hong Kong trading team that does two things well: routing re-export cargo through one of the world's busiest free ports, and running the back office for sellers shipping small commodities to the world.

From our base in Kwun Tong, we pull sourcing, quality control, customs, warehousing and last-mile into a single service — so you deal with one team, one cost view and one person who owns the result. Faster to market, clearer landed costs, fewer 3am surprises.
